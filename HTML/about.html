Background

Nduka Ikechukwu Danyel (b. 1998) is a network technician. I obtained a bachlor degree of History and International relations from Bowen University Osun in 2018. My transition into I.T began in 2020, I started out as a front-end developer as it seemed the likely path for someone like me given my educational background, I worked mainly on the developing the visual experience for users. Not long after, I began implementing back-end scripts for additional web functionalities such as RESTful APIs using python (Django framework). This experience taught me how to think critically, organize, helped me to acquire excellent teamwork abilities and I got introduced to the world of networking. I assumed training to become a network engineer at New horizons computer learning center and successful passed the CCNA certification exam. What's more, I have been working on developing and ensuring continuous deploying of network automation solutions in order to make configuration management of network topology easy.
